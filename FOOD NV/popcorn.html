Cinnamon Popcorn




I need to thank Hannah Currant for opening the world of popcorn up to me! I had never made my own before but when you compare making your own and adding your own fresh flavours to it over the stuff you buy in overpriced packets, there really aren’t many reasons not to.



Obviously salted popcorn is always a winner, and is so very very easy (just add salt!). And another great flavour idea to try is putting lemon juice and rosemary in with the salt, but for a sweet treat agave syrup and cinnamon is a winning combination.

Popcorn is so quick - it can’t take more than 5 minutes EVER, so you really have no excuse not to try making your own just once.

Enjoy

Natalie x


Makes enough for 3-4 people

  • Enough popcorn kernels to cover the base of your pan in a thin layer
  • Sunflower Oil
  • 1 tbsp agave syrup
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

  • Put the popcorn in a deep pan with some sunflower oil, put the lid on (preferably a glass/see through lid) and heat on a medium/high heat.
  • The popcorn will begin popping, shake the pan around without taking the lid off.
  • Once there is about 3 seconds between each pop take the pan off the heat.
  • Stir in the agave syrup and cinnamon tasting and adjusting the flavours if necessary.
