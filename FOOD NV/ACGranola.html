Apple and Cinnamon Granola

Having homemade granola ready in jars makes me feel like a proper grown up. I’m just ignoring the fact that these jars are old olive jars, which I buy and then eat out of with a fork like I’m eating take out, because that really isn’t a proper thing to do!

This recipe is more like a template. Just switch in and out whatever you have in the cupboard, it really it is that easy. It feels quite festive with the nutmeg and cinnamon, and cranberries are certified Christmas food right? I’m really feeling Christmas food at the moment, so I’m working on a few recipes which I can enjoy this December without feeling horrendous after. Combatting the cold with yummy food.

I like this with unsweetened almond milk, but yoghurt or normal milk is pretty good too. Enjoy

Natalie xx



  • 1 apple, chopped with skin on
  • 1/2 cup almonds
  • 1/2 cup hazelnuts
  • 1/4 cup linseeds
  • 1/2 cup cashew nuts
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 4 tbsp coconut oil, melted
  • 1 1/2 cups porridge oats
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries

Method:

  • Preheat the oven at 150°c
  • Mix the nuts, seeds, apple and spices in a baking tray
  • Add the melted coconut oil and mix so that everything is covered, then add the oats, mix again
  • Put in the oven for about an hour, the fruit should dry out, and its ok if the nuts blacken a bit, this just gives a deeper flavour.
  • Let the mix cool, add the cranberries and store it in jars or tins.
