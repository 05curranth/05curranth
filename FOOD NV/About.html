About


I’m Natalie Victoria, a 21 year old Student of History and Economics

I’m a golfer, hurdler, HUGE Springsteen fan who feels happiest on top of a ski slope or, (when that isn’t possible) mixing some ingredient or other with something else in the kitchen!

I have always created recipes, taking basic ideas and switching the ingredients about, but for my 21st birthday I was given a personalised book to write them down in, and FOOD NV whatever that is, or might be, was born!


Eating in a new way has come out of necessity to change big things in my life, and if you want to read more about that it’s below! But if not, these recipes will hopefully be vibrant, tasty, and colourful, and get your body feeling that little bit more energised and at one with you!
